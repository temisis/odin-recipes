Brigadeiro Recipe

Description

Brigadeiro is a traditional Brazilian sweet that can be eaten in little balls (like the image) or directly from a bowl, after it's ready. Either way, it's a very chocolatey-dessert, and a beloved one.

Ingredients

  • Sweetened condensed milk
  • Unsalted butter
  • Chocolate milk powder or cocoa powder, depending if you want it more or less sweet.
  • Chocolate sprinkles to garnish (if you are rolling it into balls)

Steps

  2. Mix all ingredients together, until the texture of the dough is smooth, with no apparent lumps.
